Privacy Policy & Account Data Deletion

Last updated: July 11, 2026

1. Who We Are

SoulHappy ("we", "our", "the game") is a mobile arcade game developed by an independent developer based in Malaysia. This policy explains what information SoulHappy collects, how it is used, and how you can request deletion of your data.

2. Information We Collect

SoulHappy uses Firebase and, optionally, Facebook and Google Sign-In to provide gameplay, leaderboards, and social features. Depending on how you log in, we may collect:

Data TypeWhen Collected
Anonymous device/user IDAlways (Firebase Anonymous Authentication, even in Guest mode)
Player display nameWhen you set your in-game name
Name, email address, profile photoOnly if you choose to sign in with Facebook or Google
Gameplay data (coins, scores, progress, quests, friends list)As you play, stored via Firebase/Firestore
Purchase recordsIf you make an in-app purchase (processed by Google Play; we do not receive your payment card details)

We do not collect precise location data, and we do not currently serve third-party ads. If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated accordingly before the change takes effect.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To let you play, save progress, and resume across sessions
  • To operate leaderboards, friends, and other social features
  • To process in-app purchases via Google Play
  • To maintain the security and stability of the game

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with the service providers necessary to run the game:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and data storage (servers in the Singapore/asia-southeast1 region)
  • Facebook / Google Sign-In — only if you choose to log in using these methods
  • Google Play — for processing in-app purchases

5. Data Security

All data in transit between the app and our servers is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS). Access to our backend is restricted to the developer.

6. Children's Privacy

SoulHappy is intended for players aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us using the details below so we can delete it.

🗑️ 7. Delete Your Account & Data

You can request deletion of your SoulHappy account and all associated data at any time, whether or not you continue to use the app.

How to request deletion

  • Send an email to SoulHappy2day@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include your in-game player name and, if possible, your login method (Guest / Facebook / Google) so we can locate your account

What gets deleted

We will delete your player name, saved progress, scores, quest/album/bingo data, friends list, and any linked Facebook/Google profile information stored in our systems.

Timeline

Requests are processed within 30 days. Some data may be retained briefly in backups for security purposes before being permanently purged, but will no longer be linked to an active account.
